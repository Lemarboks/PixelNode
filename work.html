School redesign

Bloubergrant High School

The challenge

The school needed an online presence that felt modern and trustworthy to parents and prospective families, instead of a dated, hard-to-navigate page that buried key information.

What we built

A clean, animated React redesign concept with a sharper landing experience, clear sections for the school's offering, and a layout that reads well on the phones most parents browse from.

The result

A presentation-ready site that communicates the school's identity at a glance — faster to scan, easier to navigate, and credible on first impression.

Fashion editorial

Sench//Index

The challenge

A street-style fashion brand needed a site that felt like an editorial magazine, not a generic store — something that carried the energy of the brand and made the imagery the hero.

What we built

A Mzansi × LDN editorial experience with bold typography, image-led fashion sections, and magazine-style navigation that puts the visuals front and centre.

The result

A distinctive, on-brand showcase that reads like a publication — the kind of presentation that makes a young fashion label look established.

Ecommerce storefront

Lynux Espadrilles

The challenge

A handmade espadrilles brand needed an online storefront that conveyed craft and quality while still making it simple for a customer to browse and buy.

What we built

A luxury-feel storefront with product storytelling, a clear shopping flow, and a brand-focused visual direction that frames the product as a premium, handmade piece.

The result

A storefront that sells the story as much as the product — positioning a small handmade brand to compete on presentation with larger names.
