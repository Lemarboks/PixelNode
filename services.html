FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does a website take?

Most landing pages and small business sites launch within 1–2 weeks once we have your content. Larger or ecommerce builds take a little longer. Need it faster? We offer rush delivery.

Do you handle hosting and domains too?

Yes. We can set up your domain, hosting, SSL, and email basics, and we run our own infrastructure — so your site stays fast and online after launch. We also offer monthly care plans that keep everything maintained.

What if I need changes after launch?

Small tweaks are easy to request. For ongoing updates, backups, and support, our monthly care plans (from R350/mo) keep your site looked after without one-off fees every time.

How does payment work?

Typically a deposit to start and the balance on completion, with monthly care plans billed monthly. We confirm the full quote before any work begins — no surprises.

Do you only work with Cape Town clients?

We're based in Cape Town and love local work, but everything we do is remote-friendly — we work with businesses and creators anywhere in South Africa and beyond.

Can you also do photography for my site?

Yes. We offer brand and product photography designed specifically for the web, so your site launches with visuals that match the quality of the build.
