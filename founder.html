The Story

From curiosity to a studio.

PixelNode began with a simple conviction: that businesses and creators deserve more than a template. Lemar Boks and Raymond Mandongwe founded the studio to bring design, engineering, and infrastructure together under one roof — so a website isn't just built and handed over, but designed, deployed, hosted, and kept running.

What started as a fascination with how websites are built grew into hands-on work across the full stack: crafting clean, fast interfaces, standing up reliable hosting, and experimenting with self-hosted servers and homelab environments. That blend — designer's eye, developer's discipline, and an operator's care for uptime — is what defines PixelNode today.

The studio now spans web design, development, hosting and domains, server and homelab setups, automation, and photography-led media — a practical, end-to-end offering for brands that want to launch properly and stay supported afterwards.
